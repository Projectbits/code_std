Version 2 Coding Standards Summary:
- Naming: snake_case (Python), PascalCase (PowerShell), kebab-case (Ansible)
- Feedback: Color-coded output (Green=Success, Yellow=Warning, Red=Error)
- Logging: File-based (e.g., logs/script.log)
- Modularity: Functions/roles
- Idempotency: State checks
